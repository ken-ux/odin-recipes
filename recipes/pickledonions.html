Pickled Onions

What are Pickled Onions?

Pickled onions are a food item consisting of onions (cultivars of Allium cepa) pickled in a solution of vinegar and salt, often with other preservatives and flavourings.

Ingredients

  • Red Onions
  • White Vinegar
  • Salt
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Thinly slice up red onions.
  2. Heat up the white vinegar, salt, and sugar in a sauce pan.
  3. Stir the vinegar mixture until the salt and sugar has dissolved. Let cool.
  4. Place sliced red onions in a container and pour vinegar mixture over the top.
