Ice Cream

What is Ice Cream?

Ice cream is a frozen dessert typically made from milk or cream that has been flavoured with a sweetener, either sugar or an alternative, and a spice, such as cocoa or vanilla, or with fruit, such as strawberries or peaches. Food colouring is sometimes added in addition to stabilizers.

Ingredients

  • Heavy Cream
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Egg Yolks
  • Whole Milk

Steps

  1. Pour heavy cream into sauce pan with sugar and salt.
  2. Warm mixture until sugar dissolves, then add in milk.
  3. Ensure mixture has cooled before mixing in egg yolks.
  4. Churn mixture in an ice cream machine.
